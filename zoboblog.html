How to make zobo

What is zobo?

Zobo is an affordable and tasty drink used as light refreshment at parties and ceremonies and even at home. Because of its health benefits, it has become popular among Nigerians. Zobo (or Zoborodo) is the word in Hausa language for the edible plant Hibiscus Sabdariffa and the popular drink it is used to make.

Ingredients

  • Hibiscus Sabdariffa leaf
  • water
  • Star anise seeds
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon sticks("Kirfa")
  • Cloves
  • Cubeb pepper("Masoro")

preparation

  1. Zobo is prepared by simply adding all the ingredients and boiling in water for some time.
  2. The herbs and spices are added one after the other and boiled for no less than 30 minutes for their contents to fully permeate the water.
  3. The solid ingredients are finally strained from the liquid using a sieve.
  4. After sieving out the solid ingredients, the liquid is poured into a plastic bottle which is used to store it. Zobo drink is often served and sold in those plastic bottles.
